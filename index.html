About

I graduated from CUNY Queens College with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science. I have many goals I want to achieve in life, and becoming a Software Engineer is one of them. With my programming skills, I want to leave a positive impact on society. Initially, I tried teaching as a possible career. After a few years, I felt it was not enough. I want to become a part of an amazing project, whether on my own or with a big company.

In my free time, I work on developing my own game and drawing art. I hope one day I will release a wonderful game and people will talk about it for years. Cave Story is my all-time favorite game. It was created by one of my greatest inspirations, Amaya Daisuke. The game was created two decades ago when technology and the internet were in their infancy. After hearing this story, I was inspired by the fact that he was able to achieve this 20 years ago. I thought if he was able to achieve this 20 years ago, then I can definitely achieve the same in the present.
